# Privacy Policy for Text Multi-Finder

**Last Updated: March 13, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Text Multi-Finder" Chrome Extension (the "Extension") collects, uses, and discloses your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent experience.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear: The "Text Multi-Finder" Chrome Extension **does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII)** from its users. We do not ask for, gather, store, or transmit any data that could be used to identify you personally, such as your name, email address, IP address, or location.

## 2. Information We Access and How It Is Used

The Extension operates locally within your browser. To provide its core functionality (multi-word highlighting and trap detection), it interacts with the content of the webpages you visit.

- **Website Content (Accessed Locally):** The Extension processes the text content of the active webpage only to identify and apply highlight styles to the keywords you have entered or to identify predefined "Trap Questions". This processing occurs entirely within your browser. **This content is not transmitted or stored on any external servers.**
- **Search Data (Local Storage):** Your search keywords, "Trap" patterns, and settings (colors, themes, etc.) are stored **locally within your browser's sync storage** (using `chrome.storage.sync`). This allows your settings to persist across your own devices where you are logged into Chrome. This data is **not transmitted to or stored on our servers.**

## 3. Permissions Used

The Extension requests the following permissions, strictly for its stated functionality:

- `scripting` & `activeTab`: Used to identify text on the webpage and apply highlight styles.
- `storage`: Used to save your highlights and settings locally.
- `contextMenus`: Used to allow you to add words to the finder via the right-click menu.
- `tabs`: Used to detect when a page has finished loading to re-apply highlights if necessary.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not share, sell, rent, or transfer any user data** (including your locally stored highlights or browsing activity) to third parties for any purpose. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to share or sell.

## 5. User Control and Data Deletion

Because your data is stored locally:

- **Access and Modification:** You can manage your keyword lists and settings directly within the extension's popup interface.
- **Deletion:** Clicking the "CLEAR" button in the extension or uninstalling the "Text Multi-Finder" Extension from your Chrome browser will remove all associated locally stored data.
