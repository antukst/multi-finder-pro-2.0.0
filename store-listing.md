## 3. Storage and Data Usage (Privacy Tab)

**Data Collection Disclosure:**

- **Product Activity:** Strictly local. The extension interacts with webpage content only to apply highlights based on user-defined keywords.
- **Personal Information:** None. The extension does not collect names, emails, addresses, or any other PII.
- **Location:** None.
- **Browsing History:** None.
- **External Transmission:** None. All data (keywords and patterns) is stored locally using `chrome.storage.sync` and is never sent to external servers.

**Justification for Permissions:**

- **`storage`**: Essential for saving user-defined keywords, custom color palettes, and interface settings across browser sessions.
- **`scripting` & **`activeTab`**: Required to programmatically identify and apply highlighting styles to the text content of the active webpage.
- **`contextMenus`**: Allows users to instantly add selected text to the finder via the right-click menu for a faster workflow.
- **`host_permissions` (`<all_urls>`)**: Enables the extension to provide its core functionality (keyword finding) on any website the user visits.

## 5. Detailed Description

**Text Multi-Finder Pro** is a high-performance, universal productivity tool designed for researchers, analysts, and professionals who need to navigate complex text data across multiple keywords simultaneously.

### 🚀 Key Features:

- **Parallel Keyword Search:** Highlight dozens of words at once with unique, vibrant colors for instant visual scanning.
- **Nano-Speed Engine:** Refactored for maximum efficiency, our new engine scans entire pages in a single pass without affecting browser speed.
- **Survey Trap Detection:** A specialized "Stealth Mode" that automatically flags common attention-check phrases and trap questions in surveys and research forms.
- **Draggable Glassmorphism UI:** A beautiful, animated floating dashboard that lets you navigate matches and move freely around the page.
- **Advanced Match Modes:** Support for Case Sensitivity, Whole Words, and "Similar" match algorithms.
- **Privacy First:** Operates 100% locally. Your keywords and browsing data never leave your device.
- **Cross-Device Sync:** Your settings and patterns are automatically synced to your Chrome profile.

### 🍱 Why Choose Pro?

Whether you're auditing long documents, analyzing code, or completing professional surveys, Text Multi-Finder Pro saves you hours by automating the search process. Its distinctive Lofi aesthetics and smooth micro-animations provide a premium experience that "standard" finders can't match.
